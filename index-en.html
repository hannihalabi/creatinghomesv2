How does it work?

Process for Styling Assignment

1. Consultation

We go through the home and look at what you need to do before the sale, and what we can help with.

2. Quote

Contains a list of measures, price, checklist, and our terms. You always receive your quote within 24h.

3. Planning

After approved quote, we plan the date for styling and photography.

4. Styling

We take care of everything ‚Äì from planning and transport to styling on site. The only thing you need to do is hand over the keys, and we'll solve the rest.

5. Photo and Viewing

After photography, the styling remains for viewing for 40 days.

6. Sold Home

You let us know when the sale is complete, and we arrange the pickup of the styling according to the agreed time.

Process for Interior Design

1. Consultation

We analyze needs, budget, and style preferences. Digitally or on site.

2. Quote

3. Planning

4. Execution
